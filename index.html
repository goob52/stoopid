Rules
The board is 14×14. A full row or column clears only when its 14 tiles form a legal hand. The game then pauses and awards line removals based on the hand: standard hand = 1, Seven Pairs = 2, and Thirteen Orphans = 13, plus one removal for every dora in the hand. You may spend those removals on any occupied rows or columns; tiles above emptied spaces fall by gravity. The dora changes after every clear. All draws come from two complete riichi sets. When a line reaches 12 tiles, the Tenpai Helper shows every tile that would make that line tenpai. In Noob Mode there is no automatic descent: Down moves one row at a time, while Space performs a hard drop.
